How to Play üïπÔ∏è

Find Snoopy is a memory and observation game. Snoopy starts by appearing under one of the three cups. Memorize his location carefully before the cups begin to shuffle.

  • Press Start Game to begin the round
  • Snoopy will appear briefly under a cup
  • The cups will shuffle positions
  • Click the cup where you think Snoopy is hiding
  • You gain a point if correct, but lose a life if wrong
  • The game speeds up every round
  • You win after surviving all rounds!
